Settings

Controls how sensitive the dashboard's spending-risk badges are (see the "Risk" column and hint on the dashboard). A grant is flagged "overspending" when its projected remaining burn rate, extrapolated to its end date, exceeds this percentage of its remaining balance, and "underspending" when that projection falls below the other percentage.

Defaults are 115% and 60%. Lower the overspending threshold or raise the underspending threshold to get flagged sooner; the underspending threshold must stay below the overspending one.
